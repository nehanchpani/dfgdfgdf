Why I Built This Gap Certificate Tool (And Saved Rs. 500)

Look, I've been there. You drop a year for JEE or NEET preparation, you study hard, and finally, when it's time for counselling, the college asks for a gap year affidavit. And we panic.

To be honest, I hate running around lawyers for something so simple. Last time, a notary guy asked me Rs. 500 just to type this standardized format for my 1 year gap certificate. I was like, really?

So, I thought, why not code it? Now, you don't need to beg anyone for the format. Whether you need a gap certificate for neet droppers or just a general education gap certificate for scholarship, this tool does the job.

How to use this for specific needs?

For NEET Counselling: If you are a dropper, select "Exam Preparation" as the reason. This format is widely accepted as a valid neet gap certificate.

For Scholarship: Many government schemes require an affidavit for gap year after 12th to ensure you weren't employed. This generated text works perfectly there too.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Q: from where to get gap certificate notarized?

Listen, this tool generates the content. You still need to print this on a Stamp Paper (usually Rs. 10 or Rs. 100 depending on your state) and get a seal from a Notary. This tool saves you the typing charges!

Q: Is this valid for MHT CET or other state counsellings?

Yes. The legal language used here is the standard gap affidavit certificate format used across India for gap certificate for mht cet and other state exams.

Q: What if I had a gap due to medical reasons?

Just select "Medical Issues" in the dropdown. The generated gap certificate after 12th will automatically reflect that you were recovering during that period.
